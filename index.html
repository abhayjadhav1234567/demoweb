Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility: Regular practice stretches and tones muscles.
  • Builds muscle strength: Yoga helps maintain a balanced muscle tone.
  • Perfects your posture: Alignment awareness leads to better posture.
  • Prevents cartilage and joint breakdown: Full range of motion exercises joints.
  • Increases blood flow: Relaxation exercises boost circulation.
  • Drops blood pressure: Helps with hypertension through better circulation.
  • Regulates adrenal glands: Lowers cortisol levels.
  • Makes you happier: Consistent practice reduces depression.

Types of Yoga

Hatha Yoga

A generic term for any type of yoga that teaches physical postures. Great for beginners.

Vinyasa Yoga

Characterized by stringing postures together so that you move from one to another seamlessly.

Ashtanga Yoga

A rigorous style of yoga that follows a specific sequence of postures and is similar to vinyasa yoga.

Iyengar Yoga

Focuses on proper alignment and uses props like blocks, straps, and chairs.

Kundalini Yoga

More spiritual than physical, this style focuses on releasing kundalini energy in your body.
